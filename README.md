# Truth Social Trending Posts Scraper

Truth Social is a social media platform there is a LOT of good reason to want to study.
This repo contains a growing list of trending posts on Truth Social,
scraped by pinging their trending posts API
every 15 minutes or so.
It is sort-of-kind-of a sample of Truth Social's content,
weightedby its prominence on the platform.
As such, it is useful for studying the sorts of content
thta people on the platform are getting exposed to.
